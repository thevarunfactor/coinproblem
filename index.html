Change Maker

  • This nifty little tool helps you calculate the minimum number of coins required to make up an amount

  • Simply enter the amount in £ or p and hit the enter/return key or click on the 'Go' button

  • Please enter only numbers. The £ or p symbols are optional -- by default, the amount will be considered as pence

  • If you input a decimal number without the £ or p symbol, it rounds to the nearest penny
